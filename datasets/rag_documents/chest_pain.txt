Chest pain can occur due to heart, lung, muscle, or digestive problems.

Common symptoms:
- Chest discomfort
- Tightness
- Pressure sensation

Possible causes:
- Angina
- Heart attack
- Acid reflux
- Muscle strain

Emergency warning signs:
- Severe chest pain
- Pain spreading to arm or jaw
- Shortness of breath
- Sweating and dizziness

Immediate medical evaluation is recommended.
