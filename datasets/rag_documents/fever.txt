Fever is a temporary increase in body temperature, usually caused by infections.

Common symptoms:
- High body temperature
- Chills
- Sweating
- Weakness
- Body pain

Possible causes:
- Viral infections
- Flu
- COVID-19
- Bacterial infections

Seek medical attention if:
- Fever is above 39°C
- Fever lasts more than 3 days
- Difficulty breathing occurs
- Patient becomes unconscious
